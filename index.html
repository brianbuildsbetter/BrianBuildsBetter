About Us

A Legacy of Quality in the Bay Area

Founded in 1998, Bay Area Builders has grown from a small family operation into one of the region's most trusted construction companies. We've worked across San Francisco, Oakland, San Jose, and everywhere in between.

Our team of licensed engineers, project managers, and skilled tradespeople takes pride in delivering on time, within budget, and beyond expectations — every single time.

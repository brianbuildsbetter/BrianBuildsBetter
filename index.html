About Us

Built on Reputation, One Project at a Time

Founded in 1998, Brian Builds Better has grown from a one-man operation into one of the Bay Area's most trusted construction companies. We've worked across San Francisco, Oakland, San Jose, and everywhere in between.

Brian and his team of licensed tradespeople take pride in delivering on time, within budget, and beyond expectations — every single time. No subcontractors you've never met. No surprises on the bill.
